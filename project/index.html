What is Veriscope?

Veriscope is a platform dedicated to the analyzing, verifying and exposing fake content, focusing on users aged 16-19 but accessible to all age groups. Our platform uses advanced AI-driven technology to verify online content, combating misinformation by analyzing texts, images, and video from diverse sources.

Veriscope collaborates with fact-checkers and verified news sources such as the British Broadcasting Corporation (BBC News), Sky News, Cable News Network (CNN) etc; contributing to transparency and ensuring that trusted information is genuinely reliable.

How It Works?



1

Capture and Upload Content

Have you come across content that seems suspicious? Simply copy and paste it in the action panel for scanning.

2

Veriscope Scans the Content

Veriscope will analyze and scan the uploaded content, giving you the probability of the uploaded content being accurate or false.

3

Other Features

You can allow Veriscope to run in the background on your device to automatically scan content. Click the overlay button in the bottom right corner to explore more features, such as the text-to-speech tool, screen recording and background content scanning. Veriscope will ask for permission to enable this features.
